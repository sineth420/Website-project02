The Onel Experience

Welcome to Onel Safari & Resort, where the wild heart of Sri Lanka beats strongest. We don't just offer safaris; we craft journeys into the soul of Udawalawa. Nestled on the borders of the national park, our resort is a sanctuary for those seeking to reconnect with nature without compromising on luxury.

Our philosophy is simple: respect nature, provide unparalleled comfort, and create memories that last a lifetime. From the moment you wake up to the sound of birdsong to the second you spot a majestic elephant herd, every detail is curated by our expert team.

Meet the Locals

Udawalawa is a sanctuary for some of the world's most magnificent creatures.

Elephants

Home to large herds of Asian elephants, often seen bathing in the reservoirs or grazing in the grasslands.

Leopards

Though elusive, these majestic predators roam the park. Our guides are skilled at spotting them.

Birdlife

A paradise for birdwatchers with over 180 species, including endemic and migratory birds.
